## About My Mentor

### PI
My PI is Professor Anhong Guo from the University of Michigan Ann Arbor. Anhong is an Assistant Professor of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Michigan in the CSE (Computer Science and Engineering) department, and he also has a courtesy appointment as Assistant Professor with the School of Information. At University of Michigan, Anhong directs the Human-AI Lab (otherwise known as HAIL), which is part of the broader [Human-Centered Computing Lab](https://hcc.engin.umich.edu/)—home to groups in the Michigan CSE Division who investigate Human Computer Interaction (HCI), Educational Technology, Multimedia, and Social Computing.
