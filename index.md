## About Me
I am a rising 4th year undergraduate at [Grinnell College](https://www.grinnell.edu/) majoring in Computer Science and Sociology with a concentration in Statistics. I have previously done research at the University of Washington Allen School as part of the [Make4All lab](https://make4all.org/) and at the Carnegie Mellon Human Computer Interaction Institute as part of the [Social AI Group](https://haiyizhu.com/) and the [CATS Lab](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~./aleven/). I am currently working with the [Human-AI Lab (HAIL)](https://guoanhong.com/), part of the University of Michigan Ann Arbor Computer Science department, as part of the CRA DREU Program.

I will be applying to PhD programs in Computer Science and HCI with an intended Fall 2025 start date, and hope to continue research in accessibility and launch a career in academia!
